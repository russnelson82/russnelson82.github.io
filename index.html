Welcome to my website. I'm currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at West Point. I recently completed my PhD in Mathematics at the University of California, Santa Barbara under Yitang Zhang. I like thinking about numbers and the stories they hide. My theoretical math research interests are in analytic number theory, and my applied math interests are in nlp, computer vision, data science, sports analytics, and cryptography. I plan to update this website with more of my projects, both applied and theoretical, so stay tuned!

Feel free to click around to learn more about my work on this website or on my github. You can also reach me at GSarajian@gmail.com for more information as well.
